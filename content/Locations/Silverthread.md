# Silverthread
#locations #silverthread #player-safe #feywild

**Type:** Fey City
**Region:** The Feywild
**Status:** Active, politically tense

---

## Overview

Silverthread is one of the oldest established cities in the Feywild — built within and around a grove of massive silver birch trees, their trunks wide as towers. The city rises in layers of platforms, buildings, and bridges that shimmer like spider silk in the shifting light.

It is not merely a city that happens to be alive. Silverthread *is* alive. The city has moods, opinions, and a long memory. Its sky reflects its emotional state. Its bridges shift when it is displeased. Residents don't speak of the city as infrastructure — they speak of it as a neighbor.

Silverthread has always been a crossroads city — neutral ground between Seelie and Unseelie influence, a place where trade, diplomacy, and uneasy coexistence have flourished for centuries. That neutrality is currently under severe strain.

## The Sky

The sky above Silverthread shifts and responds to the city's emotional state.

| Sky Appearance | City's Mood |
|---|---|
| Warm gold, settled | Content, Seelie-leaning |
| Cool violet, steady | Calm, Unseelie-leaning |
| Rose-gold and purple mixed | Uncertain, balanced |
| Rapid shifting | Anxious, stressed |
| Deep violet, darkening | Angry, violence brewing |
| Crimson flash | War, bloodshed nearby |
| Gray, flat | Grief, profound loss |

**Currently:** The sky shifts rapidly between gold and violet, settling nowhere. It hasn't been stable in weeks. Residents find this deeply unsettling.

## The Three Districts

### The Aureate — Seelie Quarter
Warm, bright, increasingly defensive. Gold and amber architecture, floral carvings, living wood details. Guards in golden armor. Home to the Seelie Archive, the Court of Petals, and the Gilt Market.

### The Umbral — Unseelie Quarter
Cool, elegant, quietly watchful. Deep purples, blacks, and silvers. Sharp angles and clean lines. Guards in violet armor. Home to the Unseelie Archive, the Dusk Chamber, and the Shadow Market.

### The Tangle — Neutral Ground
The most populated district, and the most nervous. The place where Seelie gold and Unseelie violet meet without resolving. Home to the Grand Pavilion, the Silver Bough Inn, and the Woven Cup. Everyone passes through whether they want to or not.

---

## The Bridges

Four bridges connect Silverthread's districts. There are whispers of a fifth that no one can quite account for.

- **The Goldwalk** — city gate to the Aureate. Wide, lantern-lined, welcoming.
- **The Nightpath** — city gate to the Umbral. Quieter. Violet lanterns.
- **The Silverspan** — the Tangle to the Aureate. The longest bridge in the city; glows silver at night, takes on gold tones as you approach the Aureate end.
- **The Duskwalk** — the Tangle to the Umbral. Roofed with woven branches, permanently dim.

## Notable Landmarks

**The Grand Pavilion** — The city's ceremonial heart, a vast open platform built around the base of the three largest silver birches. Currently being prepared for the Masquerade Ball.

**The Accord Stone** — A flat disc of pale grey stone at the center of the Pavilion stage. City tradition holds that any agreement made while both parties touch it is binding in Silverthread. It predates any record. Three separate factions have tried to move it. All three attempts ended with the movers waking up on the wrong side of the city with no memory of how they got there.

**The Root Shrine** — The oldest structure in the city, predating the factions. Located at the base of the oldest silver birch, in the Tangle. No formal clergy. People leave offerings.

**The Observatory** — A platform near the top of the tallest tree. Currently occupied by a scholar named Ilex, who visits every other day and returns looking more troubled each time.
