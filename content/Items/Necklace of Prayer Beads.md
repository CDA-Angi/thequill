# Necklace of Prayer Beads
#item #player-safe #luna

**Type:** Wondrous Item
**Rarity:** Rare
**Owner:** Luna
**Origin:** Gift from Rithlin Seedwind, south of Conyberry

---

A necklace of silver and blue hand-carved beads gifted to the party by Rithlin Seedwind — a wood elf druid encountered on the road south of Conyberry. He said it was intended for whoever among them could best use it.

The necklace has 6 magical beads. As a bonus action, one bead may be expended to cast its associated spell:

- **3 silver beads** — cast *Bless* (no concentration required)
- **3 blue beads** — cast *Cure Wounds* at 2nd level

Expended beads regain their magic at dawn.
