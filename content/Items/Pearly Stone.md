# Pearly Stone
#item #player-safe #luna

**Type:** Wondrous Item
**Rarity:** Uncommon
**Owner:** Luna
**Origin:** Gift from the Twig Jesters, Feywild

---

A small tear-shaped stone with a soft milky-white luminescence. Luna received it from the Twig Jesters as a reward for meeting their challenge in the Feywild.

The stone glows faintly in the presence of deception, falseness, or documents that have been magically altered. The glow is visible to anyone nearby. It does not identify the source or nature of the falseness — only that something false is present.

The party watched it react when Liraelle's program changed in her hands. That was the first time they saw it do something none of them could explain.
