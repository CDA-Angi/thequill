# Moon-Shaped Locket
#item #player-safe #luna

**Type:** Wondrous Item
**Rarity:** Rare
**Owner:** Luna
**Origin:** Bestowed in the Chamber of Echoes, Court of Echoes

---

A delicate silver locket shaped like a crescent moon, warm to the touch even in cold environments. Luna received it in the Chamber of Echoes as her personal boon — the Moonlit Bond.

Once per long rest, Luna may cast any spell she knows as though under the effects of *Sanctuary* and *Pass Without Trace* simultaneously for 1 minute, moving without sound and warded against direct targeting.

When she accepted it, she experienced a vision she has not fully spoken about. The visions have not stopped.
