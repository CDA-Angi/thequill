# Scroll of Pedigree
#item #player-safe #luna

**Type:** Scroll
**Rarity:** Common
**Owner:** Luna
**Origin:** Luna's noble family — documents her lineage as a daughter of House Heartfealia

---

A family tree diagram hand-lettered in calligraphy on high-quality vellum, bearing the crests and names of noble ancestors across multiple generations. Multiple seals of authenticity — a priest's seal and a higher nobleman's seal — confirm its legitimacy.

When presented to nobility or institutions that recognize hereditary title, the scroll establishes Luna's identity and social standing, granting advantage on Persuasion checks in those contexts — provided its authenticity is not in question.

It opens doors. It also carries history she has not chosen to discuss.
