# Ilex
#npc #silverthread #seelie #player-safe

**Species:** Eladrin
**Pronouns:** He/Him
**Age:** Unknown — appears ageless
**Occupation:** Scholar — documents emotional shifts between Seelie and Unseelie influence in the Feywild
**Location:** Shimmerleaf Inn, Silverthread
**Status:** Active

---

## At a Glance

A gentle, perceptive scholar who traveled with the party from Goldpine. He observes. He takes notes. 

---

## Appearance

Pale blue skin with a pearlescent sheen. Frost-tipped hair pulled back neatly. Elegant travel clothes slightly too fine for most places. He never removes his gloves, even to drink tea.

He tilts his head when listening.

---

## Personality

Soft-spoken, he is genuinely fascinated by his subject — the emotional life of the Feywild, the slow tipping of balance between the two Courts — and that fascination makes him perceptive about people as well as places.

He is faintly distant. He makes notes constantly, even while walking.

His last words to the party before parting at the Silverthread crossroads: *pay attention to the city, not just the people. The city.*

He is visiting the Observatory regularly. 

---

## What He Said
