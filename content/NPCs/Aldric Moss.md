# Aldric Moss
#npc #silverthread #tangle #player-safe

**Species:** Human
**Pronouns:** He/Him
**Age:** Late forties
**Occupation:** Proprietor, Silver Bough Inn
**Location:** Silver Bough Inn, The Tangle
**Status:** Active

---

## At a Glance

Your innkeeper. Broad, unhurried, unshockable. Has been standing behind the same counter for twenty years and shows no signs of stopping.

---

## Appearance

Dark hair going grey at the temples. The particular solidity of someone who has lifted too many trunks and broken up too many arguments. Always slightly in motion — setting out mugs, wiping the counter, moving through the common room like he owns it, which he does.

---

## Personality

A good innkeeper because he genuinely doesn't care which faction you belong to. He has watched Seelie and Unseelie nobles come through the Silver Bough his entire life and has noticed they all want the same things: a warm room, a hot meal, and someone who will listen. He provides all three without comment.
