# Aldric Moss
#npc #silverthread #tangle #player-safe

**Species:** Human
**Pronouns:** He/Him
**Age:** Late forties
**Occupation:** Proprietor, Silver Bough Inn
**Location:** Silver Bough Inn, The Tangle
**Status:** Active

---

## At a Glance

Your innkeeper. Unshockable, uninterested in taking sides, and better informed than he lets on.

---

## Appearance

Broad, unhurried, with the particular solidity of someone who has been standing behind the same counter for twenty years. Dark hair going grey at the temples. A permanent slight smile that means nothing in particular — just his face.

---

## Personality

Aldric is a good innkeeper because he genuinely doesn't care which faction you belong to. He has watched Seelie and Unseelie nobles come through the Silver Bough his entire life and has noticed they all want the same things: a warm room, a hot meal, and someone who will listen. He provides all three without comment.

He is also, quietly, one of the best-informed people in Silverthread. He doesn't seek out information — it finds him. People talk in common rooms. He listens.

He won't volunteer opinions about the political situation. If pressed, he'll admit he's worried.

---

## Sample Voice

> *"Rooms are upstairs, second and third doors on the left. Breakfast is at first light. Let me know if you need anything."*

> *(long pause, wiping the counter)* *"Something's off lately. People coming in, asking about things they should remember. Don't."*
