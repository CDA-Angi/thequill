# Liraelle Brightstring
#npc #silverthread #tangle #player-safe

**Species:** Fey
**Pronouns:** She/Her
**Age:** Over 400 years
**Occupation:** Musician
**Location:** Silverthread
**Status:** Active

---

## At a Glance

A musician who remembers two things that cannot both be true. She performed at the coronation. Both coronations. She was there. She knows it.

---

## Appearance

Middle-aged in appearance, though she is anything but. Exhausted to her soul. She carries a lute. 

---

## Personality

She is asking for someone to confirm that what she remembers is real.

---

## What She Knows

She performed at a coronation two hundred years ago. She has programs from that night — physical evidence, something she held in her hands. She has memories of the music she played, the room, the faces in the crowd.

She also has memories of performing at a different coronation. Two hundred years ago. The same night. A different name on the program.

Both memories are equally real to her. She cannot reconcile them. She has been trying for some time.

In front of the party, one of her programs changed. The name on it shifted from one coronation to the other while she was holding it. The seal was intact. No one touched it.

---

## What She Said

> *"I remember both. I know that's not possible. I remember both anyway."*
