# Commander Veth Arran
#npc #silverthread #citywatch #player-safe

**Species:** Half-elf
**Pronouns:** She/Her
**Age:** Mid-forties
**Occupation:** Commander, Silverthread City Watch
**Location:** The Tangle — Watch station and patrols
**Status:** Active

---

## At a Glance

Not corrupt. Just overwhelmed. Running double patrols with an understaffed force in a city tightening toward violence.

---

## Appearance

Warm brown skin, dark hair cut short for practicality. Silver armor that needs a polish she hasn't had time for. She moves like someone always calculating the fastest route between two points. She is not tall. She doesn't need to be.

---

## Personality

Good at her job in the way people who have been doing a hard job for a long time get good at it — not flashy, just reliably effective and honest about what she can and can't do. She considers the Watch's neutrality a professional obligation, not a political choice. Both factions have tried to pressure her. Both have found her unresponsive in a polite-but-absolutely-final way.

She will be useful to the party if they treat her as a professional. She decides which category people fall into quickly and accurately.

---

## Sample Voice

> *"I have forty-three Watch officers for a city of several thousand, two factions actively trying to use my people as proxies, and a masquerade ball coming. Tell me quickly."*

> *"Both sides have asked me to do things I won't do. That's how I know I'm doing my job correctly."*

> *"The arguments have changed lately. People aren't just disagreeing — they're confused. I don't have an explanation for that yet. I'd like one."*
