# Commander Veth Arran
#npc #silverthread #citywatch #player-safe

**Species:** Half-elf
**Pronouns:** She/Her
**Age:** Mid-forties
**Occupation:** Commander, Silverthread City Watch
**Location:** The Tangle — Watch station and patrols
**Status:** Active

---

## At a Glance

Running double patrols with an understaffed force in a city tightening toward violence.

---

## Appearance

Warm brown skin, dark hair cut short for practicality. Silver armor. Compact, purposeful — she moves like someone who knows exactly where she's going.

---

## Personality

She considers the Watch's neutrality a professional obligation, not a political choice. Both factions have tried to pressure her. Both have found her unresponsive in a polite-but-absolutely-final way.

She will be useful to the party if they treat her as a professional. She decides which category people fall into quickly and accurately.
